About LEKE'S FOUNDATION

Our Mission, Vision & Values

LEKE'S FOUNDATION is dedicated to providing accessible, high-quality healthcare to our community. Founded in 2010, our hospital has grown into a trusted center for medical excellence, compassion, and innovation. We believe in putting patients first, investing in advanced technology, and supporting our staff to deliver the best care possible.

  • Our Mission: To deliver patient-centered healthcare with excellence in quality, service, and access.
  • Our Vision: To be the leading healthcare provider, recognized for clinical excellence, innovation, and community partnership.
  • Our Values: Compassion, Integrity, Teamwork, Innovation, and Respect for all.
What Sets Us Apart?
  • 24/7 Emergency and Critical Care
  • State-of-the-art Diagnostic and Surgical Facilities
  • Comprehensive Outpatient and Inpatient Services
  • Community Health Outreach and Education
  • Multidisciplinary Team of Experienced Professionals
